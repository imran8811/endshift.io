Who are We

Empowering your ideas with machine intelligence, thus enabling you to take decisions that really matter

Read More

EndShift is a family of developers, designers, engineers and entrepreneurs dedicated to push the boundaries of digital world

It all began in a coffee shop (like all good things), where a bunch of nerds was meeting for their monthly gossip session. One thing led to another and by the end of a five hour session and four mugs down, EndShift was resurrected. Resurrected because we all once dreamt of establishing a ‘one stop digital shop’, a concept which was relatively new at that time, and most of us had forgotten about it, until five years down the lane the idea was re-hatched and all were on-board.
It has been three years since the inception of EndShift and things couldn’t have been any better, each day we are faced with a new challenge, a fresh cliffhanger, we get to meet new people who are passionate about their idea and we help them convert their ideas into reality. Laying our hands on cutting edge technologies and coning new marketing terms is what keeps us going.
We particularly look forward to work with people who are as passionate about their product as we are. Working with newly founded companies and startups gives us pure joy, as we get to play around with fresh ideas and this has helped us grow both as professionals and individuals. Our long list of satisfied clients is testimony to our hard work and sheer dedication. As a fairly fresh company ourselves
we still possess the same level enthusiasm which we had during our first venture. Digital arena is changing swiftly and we too are constantly evolving with it, thanks to our diverse set of clients, who always keep us on our toes!
